# Q3–Q5 notes

## Q3: Why is the number of companies not always 500?

The S&P 500 is reconstituted periodically; constituents are added and removed. Data may also have more than 500 names in some months (e.g. 506) due to timing of additions and deletions. So the count varies (min=500, max=506 in this sample).
